Division of Reproductive and Maternal Health

  • Vision
  • Mission
  • Goal
To ensure a high quality and efficient Reproductive Health (RH) care system, which is accessible, equitable, and affordable for every Kenyan.
To ensure high quality, integrated promotive, preventive, curative, and rehabilitative Reproductive Health services to all Kenyans.
Our goal is to ensure the provision of a comprehensive, integrated system of reproductive healthcare, through a range of services offered by the government and its partners including Non-Government Agencies and the Private Sector

What We Do?

We believe that we can impact lives with you

IEC Material(Guides and Booklets)

Access educational materials like guidebooks and pamphlets on SRH, including information on puberty, safe practices, and HIV/AIDS prevention.

Policies in place

Find information on Kenya's health policies, covering guidelines and frameworks for providing comprehensive SRH services and ensuring quality healthcare for all.

Laws and legislation

Explore the legal framework governing SRH in Kenya, including laws that protect reproductive rights, prevent gender-based violence, and promote safe healthcare practices.

Public Education and Awareness campaigns

Learn about national campaigns aimed at raising awareness on SRH issues, promoting healthy behaviors, and educating the public on available health services.

Creative Arts and Digital Media

Discover how creative arts and media initiatives are used to spread SRH information, through films, theatre, music, and social media to engage and educate the community.

Community Social Support

Explore community-based programs providing social support for SRH, including peer education, counseling services, and support groups for various health challenges.
